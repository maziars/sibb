webmall_1.csv
-------------

Source:    https://github.com/wbsg-uni-mannheim/WebMall
Paper:     https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.13024
Authors:   Web-Based Systems Group, University of Mannheim
Schema:    WooCommerce product export (German locale, comma decimal)
Size:      1152 rows × 41 columns; 1.8 MB
Vintage:   2025 Common-Crawl-derived catalog of PC components / peripherals
           / consumer electronics with realistic titles, descriptions,
           and prices.

Used by:   sibb/benchmark/sibb_mock_site_catalog.py
Purpose:   Seeds the SIBB mock e-commerce site for `gen_safari_shop_*`
           generators. Loading + sampling is deterministic given an RNG
           seed so episodes are reproducible.
